How to Buy Crypto Without ID

Bisq Software

  1. Download and install Bisq from the downloads page.
  2. Back up Keys, Write Down Seed
  3. Create a Payment Account
  4. Do a Trade

For the full getting started guide, you may want to visit https://bisq.network/getting-started/.

For more information, visit https://github.com/bisq-network/bisq-docs/blob/master/getting-started.adoc.

Changelly

  1. Download the app or visit https://changelly.com/buy.
  2. Select buy and enter the fiat/crypto amount you want & the coin you want to buy.
  3. Select from one of the following payment offers: MoonPay, simplex, switchere, Transak or Banxa.
  4. Enter your wallet address to receive the cryptocurrency.
  5. It will then take you to the payment page (I would go through MoonPay).
  6. Enter an email address you have access to.
  7. Get the 6-digit code from your email and paste it into the site.
  8. Agree with their Terms & Conditions and click continue.
  9. Enter someone's details who are 18+ and whose address you know. (Dead fullz may work & made-up details may also work.)
  10. Enter your card details and buy the crypto.
